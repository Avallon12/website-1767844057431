Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a cauliflower crust that is 100% gluten-free. However, please note that we work in a kitchen with flour, so cross-contamination is possible.

What is your delivery radius?

We deliver within a 5-mile radius of our downtown location. For larger catering orders, we can travel further.

Are there vegan options?

Absolutely. We have vegan cheese available and plenty of vegetable toppings.
